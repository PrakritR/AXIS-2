# Privacy Policy

The privacy policy is maintained in one place: the live `/privacy` page,
rendered from [`src/app/(public)/privacy/page.tsx`](../src/app/(public)/privacy/page.tsx)
(production URL: https://www.axis-seattle-housing.com/privacy — the URL to give
external services such as App Store Connect and Twilio A2P registration). The
companion SMS program terms live at `/sms-terms`
([`src/app/(public)/sms-terms/page.tsx`](../src/app/(public)/sms-terms/page.tsx)).

Edit the page components, not this file — a markdown copy kept here drifted
out of date and was removed.
