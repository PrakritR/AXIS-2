# Privacy Policy — Axis

_Last updated: June 28, 2026_

Axis ("we," "us," "our") provides property management software operated by Axis
Seattle Housing. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect
information when you use the Axis website and mobile apps (iOS and Android).

## Information we collect

- **Account information:** name, email address, phone number, and role (manager
  or resident) when you create or are invited to an account.
- **Property & tenancy data:** lease details, payment history, maintenance
  requests, documents, and messages you submit through the service.
- **Payment information:** rent and subscription payments are processed by
  Stripe. We do not store full card or bank account numbers on our servers.
- **Device information (mobile app):** push notification tokens to deliver alerts
  you opt into; camera and photo access only when you choose to attach images to
  documents or work orders.
- **Usage data:** standard logs (IP address, browser/device type, pages visited)
  and product analytics (pages viewed, clicks, and feature usage, captured via
  PostHog) to operate, secure, and improve the service.

## How we use information

- Provide and improve the Axis platform
- Process payments and send transaction confirmations
- Send email, SMS, and push notifications related to your account (e.g. rent
  reminders, messages)
- Authenticate users and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations

## Sharing

We share data with service providers who help us operate Axis, including
Supabase (authentication and database), Stripe (payments), Twilio (SMS), Resend
(email), Firebase (push notifications), and PostHog (product analytics). **We do
not sell your personal information.**

## Your choices

- You may update profile information in the app.
- Push notifications can be disabled in your device Settings.
- Residents: contact your property manager for account closure or data questions
  related to your tenancy.

## Data retention

We retain information as long as your account is active or as needed to provide
the service and meet legal requirements.

## Security

We use industry-standard measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and
access controls. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## Children

Axis is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect
information from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy. We will post the revised version at this URL with an
updated date.
