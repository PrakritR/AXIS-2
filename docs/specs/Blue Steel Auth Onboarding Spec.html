02

The auth scene

One scene, reused by every page: full chrome substrate (all four layers) behind a single floating glass AuthCard, with the PublicNavbar on top and PublicFooter below. This is a marquee moment — keep it dark by default.

  • AuthCard: 460px max, 24px radius, frosted glass (blur(24px)), 22–32% white border, inset top highlight, deep ambient shadow. Replace today's solid white card background with the glass fill.
  • Title: display weight 600, navy on light / white on dark. Optional accent fragment uses the clipped gradient.
  • Fields: map the existing --auth-input-bg to the glass field fill — rgba(255,255,255,.10) on dark, rgba(255,255,255,.66) on light — with a visible ≥1px border and cobalt focus ring (--ring).
  • Submit: full-width metallic (dark) / cobalt→sky (light) pill. Secondary links ("Forgot password?", "Sign in") use the accent color.

The legibility wash keeps the card edge and label text readable over the moving metal — required, not optional.

03

Onboarding specials

manager-id — reserved ID
Show the Axis Manager ID in a mono, monospace chip with a copy button (glass, accent on copy-confirm). The 3-step progress strip reuses the cobalt→sky gradient bar + numbered circles from the portal wizard rail.
choose-portal — role cards
Each portal (admin / property / resident / owner) is a glass card with an accent dot, label, and short description. Hover lifts with a cobalt-tinted shadow; selected gets the cobalt gradient border.
continue — router loading
Minimal: centered logo tile + a small steel-light spinner on the chrome. No card needed; it redirects within a beat.
create-account — role variants
One card, copy varies by ?role=. When session_id/slot are present, show a small confirmed-context chip above the form (glass + accent dot).
04

Do & don't

DO
  • Keep one centered card on full chrome.
  • Keep the legibility wash behind the card.
  • Honor the user's theme + OS preference.
  • Use real focus rings and labeled fields.
DON'T
  • Don't stack multiple cards over the metal.
  • Don't run long multi-field forms here — link out.
  • Don't lose contrast on field borders.
  • Don't add a second accent hue.
